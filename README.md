# Privacy Policy for Sổ Đơn

**Last updated:** 2026-01-07

## Introduction

Sổ Đơn ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy.

Sổ Đơn is designed to work **offline-first** for all core features.
To support the development and maintenance of the app, Sổ Đơn may display **third-party advertisements**.

All data handling is described transparently in this Privacy Policy.

---

## Data Collection

### User Data

Sổ Đơn **does not collect or store user-entered personal data on external servers**.

All data entered by users, including:

* Orders
* Products
* Customers
* Images added to orders or products

are stored **locally on the user's device only**.

We do not have access to this data.

---

### Advertising Data

The app may display advertisements provided by **third-party advertising services**.

These services may automatically collect limited information, such as:

* Device information (e.g. model, OS version)
* Advertising identifiers
* Approximate location (non-precise)
* App interaction data related to advertisements

This data is collected and processed **by the advertising providers**, not by Sổ Đơn, and is used for purposes such as:

* Displaying advertisements
* Measuring ad performance
* Preventing fraud and abuse

---

## Backup & Restore

Sổ Đơn provides manual backup and restore features.

* Backup files are created only when the user explicitly requests
* Backup files are stored locally or in a user-selected location
* The app does not upload or access backup files

All backup and restore operations are performed entirely on the user’s device.

---

## Permissions

The app may request the following permissions to support core features:

### Camera

Used only to allow users to take photos (e.g. product or order images).
Photos are stored locally on the device and are **not uploaded or shared by the app**.

### Photos / Media / Storage access

Used to:

* Select images from the device gallery
* Save app data locally
* Export or restore backup files

These permissions are **optional** and are requested **only when the user explicitly performs related actions**.

---

## External Links

The app may open external applications such as web browsers or email clients
when the user chooses to contact support or view related information.

No user data is automatically shared by Sổ Đơn with external services.

---

## Third-party Services

Sổ Đơn uses **third-party advertising services** to display ads.

These services may include, but are not limited to:

* **Google AdMob**

Third-party services may collect and process data in accordance with their own privacy policies.
Sổ Đơn does not control and is not responsible for the data practices of these third parties.

---

## Children’s Privacy

Sổ Đơn is **not directed to children under the age of 13**.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
Advertisements shown in the app are **not intended to be targeted at children**.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so appropriate action can be taken.

---

## Data Security

All user data is stored locally on the user's device.

We do not operate servers that store user data and do not have remote access to it.
Users are responsible for protecting access to their devices and any exported backup files.

---

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
Continued use of the app after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
